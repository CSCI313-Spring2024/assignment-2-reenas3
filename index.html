About Me

From my early childhood, a profound passion for the ever-evolving world of technology sparked within me. This fascination fueled my determination to pursue a path in the realm of computer science. Currently enrolled as a junior at North Dakota State University, I am actively engaged in learning and honing my skills in this dynamic field. Join me on this journey, where a childhood passion evolves into a lifelong pursuit of knowledge and success. Together, let's shape a future where technology meets creativity.
